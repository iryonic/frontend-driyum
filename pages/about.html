OUR JOURNEY

From a small kitchen experiment to revolutionizing snacking

2020

The Beginning

Started in a small home kitchen with a simple idea: what if healthy snacks could taste incredible? Our first batch of banana chips sold out in 24 hours.

2021

First Breakthrough

Launched our Galactic Banana Blast, which became an instant hit. Featured in "Top 10 Healthy Snacks" by Food Magazine.

2022

Going National

Expanded to 50+ stores across India. Reached 10,000 happy customers and launched our sustainable packaging initiative.

2023

Innovation Hub

Opened our R&D center with food scientists and nutritionists. Launched 15 new flavors and achieved carbon-neutral certification.

2024

The Future

Launching interstellar flavors and expanding globally. Working on space-age packaging and zero-waste initiatives.
