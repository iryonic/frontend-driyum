Frequently Asked Questions

Is this product really vegan?

Absolutely! Our Galactic Banana Blast is 100% plant-based. We use only bananas, coconut oil, and natural seasonings. No animal products or by-products are used in any stage of production.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders are delivered within 2-3 business days. We offer express shipping options at checkout for faster delivery.

What's the shelf life?

Unopened packages have a shelf life of 6 months. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 3 months for optimal freshness.

Can I return the product if I don't like it?

Yes! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, contact our customer support team for a full refund or exchange.

Yes! Our Galactic Banana Blast is kid-friendly and makes a healthy alternative to traditional chips. However, as with any snack, it should be consumed as part of a balanced diet.
